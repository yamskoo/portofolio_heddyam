A quick intro

Who am I?

This page skips the buzzwords. Welcome to the space where I talk simply about who I am, my journey, my passions, and what drives me day to day.

Discover ↓

Where my mindset comes from

Coming from a family of shopkeepers, I grew up with a strong sense of work and perseverance. From summer jobs to pratical internships, I learned to keep moving forward while embracing effort.

This mindset still guides me today — personally and professionally. It taught me essential values: rigor, accountability, and responsiveness when things don’t go as planned.

Personality

Smiling, energetic, and competitive by nature, I love taking on challenges — both personal and collective. My curiosity and drive to learn push me to explore new horizons, whether in sports, projects, or passions.

Always committed and persistent, I strive to give my best in what I do. That attitude has found its place and been recognized in the companies where I’ve worked.

Sport, a major driver

Sport has always been part of my life and fuels my taste for challenge. From competitive football to my first big personal goals, I’ve learned to push myself step by step: crossing Réunion Island on foot (GRR2), Paris–Deauville by bike, several multi-day self-supported treks… My biggest achievement so far is the Paris Marathon, finished in 2:57 (Top 2%).

Today, between running and cycling, I’m aiming for new horizons: complete an Ironman, run an ultra-trail, and chase the mythical 2:30 in the marathon.

Other passions

Beyond sport, I find as much joy in photography and video — I love capturing travel moments and landscapes. Cinema and reading also matter a lot; my bookshelf is growing and I’m catching up on the classics (promise!).

In a different vein, I’m really into transport — aerospace, rail, automotive—fields that fascinate me for their technology, innovation and their real-world impact on how we live and move.

Travel to grow

Travel is a source of inspiration and freedom for me. My exchange semester in Ireland left a mark: unique landscapes, people, and discovering a new culture— I learned as much about myself as I did about the country.

Since then, I’ve kept that taste for elsewhere and the urge to discover more — through new countries, languages, or simply different ways of seeing life. Every trip becomes a human adventure and a source of personal growth.

In short

Sport drives me, travel opens my mind, and my passions bring balance and creativity. Together, they shape my personality, my energy, and how I approach each project—both professional and personal.

And to say more, let the photos speak.
